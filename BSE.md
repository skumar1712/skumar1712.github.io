# Black--Scholes equation


The function \( u(x,t) \) satisfies the heat equation.
$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \left( \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} \right), \quad (x,y)\in \Omega,\; t>0
$$
